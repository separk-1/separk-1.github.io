Seongeun Park

Hello, I'm deeply interested in the application of deep learning in construction automation.

I am currently researching methods to bridge the gap between laboratory and real sites through data generation and domain adaptation.

Research Interests

I have a keen interest in various research utilizing deep learning. I am studying diversely!

  • Human Activity Recognition(HAR) with Domain Adaptation

    I have addressed the performance degradation problem that occurs when the target worker is not included in the source worker, a limitation of conventional construction HAR research, through a variational autoencoder. I am challenging to develop a general model that can actually be applied to construction sites.

  • Computer Vision

    I have collected datasets and developed models to detect heavy objects, a serious hazard factor on construction sites. In the future, I will challenge to develop a general model using data generation technologies like stable diffusion.

  • Human-Robot Interaction

    Although future construction will be robot-based, collaboration with humans is inevitable before full automation. I am interested in HAR research through robot vision in situations of collaboration with humans by applying the technology I have to robots.

  • Natural Language Processing(NLP)

    I participated in research to develop a model that infers possible accident types based on work plans. I think that fine-tuning chat GPT appropriately will also have various applications in the construction field in the future.
